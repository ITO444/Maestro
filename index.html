Food Waste Facts

Educate yourself and share these facts with your friends & family.

  • 3,337 tons of food waste are disposed of in landfills each day in Hong Kong. This is equivalent to the weight of 7.3 fully-loaded Boeing 747s.

  • Roughly 1/3 of the food produced in the world for human consumption every year — approximately 1.3 billion tonnes — gets lost or wasted.
